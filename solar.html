Sun

The Sun is a giant ball of hot plasma at the center of our solar system. It contains 99.8% of all the mass and keeps planets in orbit.

  • Type: G-type main-sequence star
  • Diameter: 1,391,000 km
  • Temperature: ~5,500°C (surface)

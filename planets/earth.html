Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun, and the only one known to support life. Abundant water, a protective atmosphere, and a magnetic field make Earth unique in the Solar System.

  • Distance from Sun: ~150 million km
  • Diameter: 12,742 km
  • Orbital period: 365.25 days
  • Mean surface temperature: ~15°C
  • Moons: 1 (Moon, "Luna")
  • Key feature: Only planet with liquid water and life

Quick Facts

Earth's atmosphere is about 78% nitrogen and 21% oxygen. The Moon stabilizes Earth's tilt, influencing tides and seasons. Home to 8 million+ known species, it's the only confirmed world with a biosphere.
